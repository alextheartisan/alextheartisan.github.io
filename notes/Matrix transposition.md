# Matrix transposition

$$
	{\A_{nm}}^T = \A_{mn}
$$

$$
\BM
	a & \Red{b} \\ \Blue{c} & d
\EM
^T
=
\BM
	a & \Blue{c} \\ \Red{b} & d
\EM
$$

$$
\BM
	a &	\Red{b} & \Blue{c} \\
	\Green{d} & e & \Violet{f} \\
	\Orange{g} & \Cyan{h} & i
\EM
^T
=
\BM
	a &	\Green{d} & \Orange{g} \\
	\Red{b} & e & \Cyan{h} \\
	\Blue{c} & \Violet{f} & i
\EM
$$
