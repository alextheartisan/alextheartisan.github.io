# Angles

## Types:

- Acute angle: 1-89 deg
- Right angle: 90 deg
- Obtuse angle: 91-179 deg
- Straight angle: 180 deg

* Adjacent angle
* Vertical angle


## Converting between radians and degrees

$$
\BA
	\text{1 rad} & = 
	\frac{180}{\pi}	\ \text{deg} \approx \text{57.29578 deg}  \\[1em]
	\text{1 deg} & = 
	\frac{\pi}{180} \ \text{rad} \approx \ \text{0.01745329 rad}
\EA
$$
