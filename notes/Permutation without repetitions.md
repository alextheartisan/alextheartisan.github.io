# Permutation without repetitions

$$
	P_n = n!
$$
$$
	P^r_n = n!
$$

$$
	P^2_3 = 3! = 6
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	P^3_3 = 3! = 6
$$

$$
\begin{alignedat}{2}
	\{A, B, C\} \to \ & \{A, B\} 
	\quad \quad
	\{A, B, C\} \to \ & \{A, B, C\} 
	\\
	& \{B, A\} & \{A, C, B\} \\
	& \{A, C\} & \{B, A, C\} \\
	& \{C, A\} & \{B, C, A\} \\
	& \{B, C\} & \{C, A, B\} \\
	& \{C, B\} & \{C, B, A\}
\end{alignedat}
$$



### Notes

- A standard deck of cards has $$52! = 8.06 \cdot 10^{67}$$ different permutations
