# Cartesian product

$$
Set_{\{x\}} \times Set_{\{y\}} \rightarrow Set_{\{x, y\}}
$$


$$
A \times B
$$

$$
\BA
\{A, B\} \times \{C, D\} \rightarrow \ &\{A, C\} \\
& \{A, D\} \\
& \{B, C\} \\
& \{B, D\}
\EA
$$

$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
	\times & A & B \\
	C & \{A,C\} & \{B,C\} \\
	D & \{A,D\} & \{B,D\} \\
\end{array}
$$

### Properties

$$
	A \times B \neq B \times A
$$

### Examples 

- a deck of cards
- a 2D-coordinate system
