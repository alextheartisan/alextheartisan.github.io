# Vector · Scalar operation

$$
	Vector \cdot Scalar \to Vector
$$

$$
\v {\color{purple} x}
=
\BM v_1 \\ v_2 \EM{\color{purple} x}
=
\BM v_1 \color{purple} x \\ v_2 \color{purple} x \EM
$$
