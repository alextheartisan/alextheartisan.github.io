# Cartesian product

$$
Set_{\{x\}} \times Set_{\{y\}} \rightarrow Set_{\{x, y\}}
$$

$$
\BA
\{a, b\} \times \{c, d\} \rightarrow \ &\{a, c\} \\
& \{a, d\} \\
& \{b, c\} \\
& \{b, d\}
\EA
$$

$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
	\times & a & b \\
	c & \{a,c\} & \{b,c\} \\
	d & \{a,d\} & \{b,d\} \\
\end{array}
$$

Examples: a deck of cards, a 2D-coordinate system
