# Vector magnitude

$$
    \|\vec{\Green v}|| =
    \sqrt{v_1^2 + v_2^2 + \ldots + v_n^2} =
    \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n} v_i^2}
$$

::vectormagnitude

For a 2D vector:

$$
    \|\vec{\Green v}\| = \sqrt{v_1^2 + v_2^2}
$$

For a 3D vector:

$$
    \|\vec{\Green v}\| = \sqrt{v_1^2 + v_2^2 + v_3^2}
$$

### Properties

$$
\BA
	\|\v\| & \geq 0 \\
	\|\v\| & = 0 \ \text{if} \ \v = [0, 0, \ldots, 0] \\
	\|\x\v\| & = |\x|\|\v\| \\
	\|\v + \w\| & \leq \|v\| + \|\w\|
\EA
$$
