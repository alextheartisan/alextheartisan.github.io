# Permutation with repetitions

$$
	\bar{P}^r_n = n^r
$$

$$
	\bar{P}^2_3 = 3^2 = 9 
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\quad
	\bar{P}^3_3 = 3^3 = 27
$$

$$
\begin{alignedat}{2}
	\{A, B, C\} \to \ & \{A, A\} 
	\quad \quad 
	\{A, B, C\} \to \ & \{A, A, A\} \\ 
	
	& \{A, B\} & \{A, A, B\} \\
	& \{A, C\} & \{A, A, C\} \\
	& \{B, A\} & \{A, B, A\} \\
	& \{B, B\} & \{A, B, B\} \\
	& \{B, C\} & \{A, B, C\} \\
	& \{C, A\} & \{A, C, A\} \\
	& \{C, B\} & \{A, C, B\} \\
	& \{C, C\} & \{A, C, C\} \\
	
	&& \{B, A, A\} \\
	&& \{B, A, B\} \\
	&& \{B, A, C\} \\
	&& \{B, B, A\} \\
	&& \{B, B, B\} \\
	&& \{B, B, C\} \\
	&& \{B, C, A\} \\
	&& \{B, C, B\} \\
	&& \{B, C, C\} \\
	
	&& \{C, A, A\} \\
	&& \{C, A, B\} \\
	&& \{C, A, C\} \\
	&& \{C, B, A\} \\
	&& \{C, B, B\} \\
	&& \{C, B, C\} \\
	&& \{C, C, A\} \\
	&& \{C, C, B\} \\
	&& \{C, C, C\} \\	
\end{alignedat}
$$
