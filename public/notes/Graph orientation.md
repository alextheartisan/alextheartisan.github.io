# Graph orientation

Graph can be _oriented_, _unoriented_ and _mixed_

*Oriented graph*

$$
G = (V, E)
$$

::insertion[graph]{type=oriented}

*Unoriented graph*

$$
G = (V, A)
$$

::insertion[graph]{type=unoriented}

*Mixed graph*

$$
G = (V, E, A)
$$

::insertion[graph]{type=mixed}
