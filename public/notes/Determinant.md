# Determinant

Determinant denoted as $\Delta$ or $\det$

$$
\Delta =
\det \BM
	\Green m_{11} & \Blue m_{12} \\
	\Orange m_{21} & \Violet m_{22}
\EM
=
\Green m_{11} \Violet m_{22} - \Blue m_{12} \Orange m_{21}
$$
