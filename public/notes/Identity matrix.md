# Identity matrix

Square matrix with ones on the _main diagonal_

$$
\begin{align}
    I_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} &&
    I_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
\end{align}
$$

Properties

$$
\A I = I \A = \A
$$
