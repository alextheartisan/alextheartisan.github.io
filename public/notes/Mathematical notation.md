# Mathematical notation

## Sum

$$
\sum^n_{i=1} a_i =
a_1 +
a_2 +
\ldots +
a_{n-1} +
a_{n}
$$

## Product

$$
\prod^n_{i=1} a_i =
a_1 \times
a_2 \times
\ldots \times
a_{n-1} \times
a_{n}
$$

## Intervals

$$
\BA
	[a, b] & = a \leq x \leq b
	\\
	(a, b) & = a \lt x \lt b
	\\
	[a, b) & = a \leq x \lt b
	\\
	(a, b] & = a \lt x \leq b
\EA
$$
