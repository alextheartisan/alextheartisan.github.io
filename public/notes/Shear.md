# Shear

2D

$$
H_x(\lambda) =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & \lambda \\ 0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
;
H_y(\lambda) =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\ \lambda & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
H(\lambda, \mu) =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & \lambda \\ \mu & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

3D

$$
H_{xz}(\lambda) =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & \lambda \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
